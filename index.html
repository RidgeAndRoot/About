I’m Ben Thompson, an Australian designer that studied at Swinburne University of Technology.

I was lucky to have been nominated for Swinburnes' BIM Award in my final year of study, where I got passionate about designing, construction documentation, generative design and computational scripting. After 3 years of study, I've been working on a project in collaboration with family members to deliver design opportunities for country living. I’ve also been developing new skills and challenging myself to come up with new concepts every day that combine design, scripts and code.

As a designer, I’ve needed to overcome some barriers in my career. It’s taught me to push through and stand up for myself. I want to inspire other designers to do the same, so don’t hesitate to reach out. I’m always online.
